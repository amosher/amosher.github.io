What is America's Guest

Americas Golf Club was founded by two guys who met 35 years ago in Kindergarten. Kindergarten sat in alphabetical order in their town (Mosher, Northrup). One went on to become an Engineer (Pete Mosher) who dreams of retiring and playing golf as much as humanly possible and the other went on to become a Class A Golf Pro who has taught countless people to play better golf.

They remain close friends to this day and they have a dream where they can provide a way for those that are retired to play the game they love without being hindered by how much it costs. And golfers need variety so you shouldn’t be limited to just playing 1 course all year long. To solve this problem, Jake and Pete created Americas Golf Club which provides membership packages to those over 55 to up to four local courses in their area at a significant discount from the published senior rates. Unfortunately the number of available memberships is not unlimited because of the low price these memberships are offered at. To join our waiting list for a membership Click Here

How it Works

Americas Golf Club has negotiated very low prices for a specific number of golf rounds per season from courses in your area. Golf Courses who participate benefit because you are using tee times that would otherwise go unfilled. It is a win-win for everyone. The benefit to you is very low prices for those of you take advantage of a seasonal memberships with America’s Golf Club. All golf memberships are for tee times Monday through Friday before 3pm when all those younger people are working for the man.

Available Membership Tiers:

  • 20 rounds (up to 2 courses)
  • 30 rounds (up to 3 courses)
  • 40 rounds (up to 4 courses)
  • 50 rounds (up to 4 courses)
  • 60 rounds (up to 4 courses)
